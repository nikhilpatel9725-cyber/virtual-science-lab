Heat Transfer

Aim

To study heat transfer by conduction, convection and radiation.

Theory

Heat is transferred from a hotter body to a colder body. There are three modes of heat transfer:

  • Conduction – through solids
  • Convection – through liquids and gases
  • Radiation – without any medium

Requirements

PhET Simulation, computer/mobile, internet connection

Procedure

  1. Open the simulation.
  2. Select heat source and material.
  3. Observe temperature changes.

Simulation

Observation

Heat flows from hot object to cold object.

Result

Heat transfer occurs by conduction, convection and radiation.
